# Opening number

## Song structure

1. Chorus \(solo\)
2. Chorus \(tutti\) 
3. Verse
4. Chorus
5. Bridge
6. Chorus

The form above is also known as "the one and one" \(one verse, one bridge\).

For class shows, you may also see this form which features an extra verse.

1. Chorus \(solo\)
2. Chorus \(tutti\) 
3. Verse 1
4. Chorus
5. Verse 2
6. Chorus
7. Bridge
8. Chorus
